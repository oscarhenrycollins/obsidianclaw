Two tools, one workflow.

Obsidian organizes your thinking. OpenClaw gives you an AI agent that acts on it. ObsidianClaw connects them — your agent reads your notes, writes new ones, and chats with you without leaving your vault.

From Obsidian

  • Beautiful Markdown rendering
  • Vault as single source of truth
  • End-to-end encrypted sync
  • Works offline, files are yours
  • Massive plugin ecosystem
+

From OpenClaw

  • Autonomous AI agent
  • Runs tools, searches the web
  • Reads and writes your files
  • Multi-channel (Telegram, Signal, etc.)
  • Local-first, privacy-respecting
